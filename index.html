The PROs When Hiring Whitelabel SEO Agency

1. Speed and Efficiency

When selecting an agency to work with, it’s important to ensure they have the necessary speed and manpower to complete the job or project efficiently. The agency should have a large team with ample resources to finish the work as quickly as possible.

Specifically, you’ll want to confirm that the agency has a significant number of skilled personnel who can dedicate the required time and effort to your project. With a well-staffed team, the agency will be able to maintain a swift pace and deliver the final results in a timely manner.

2. Access to Team of Experts

When looking for a Whitelabel agency, ensuring they have a team of experts in their respective fields is important. Some Whitelabel agencies may only have a small staff, which can make it challenging to scale your business effectively.

If you’re seeking a Whitelabel SEO agency, make sure they offer a comprehensive suite of services, such as content creation, technical optimization, on-page optimization, link building, and reporting. Having specialists in each of these areas will allow you to scale your SEO efforts more easily without running into problems.

Many agencies claim to provide full-service SEO, but it’s crucial to verify that they have the necessary expertise in each component of SEO. This will ensure you can efficiently grow your business without encountering roadblocks along the way.

3. Focus on your Strength

If you’re working with a Whitelabel agency, you can focus on your strengths. For example, if your strength is getting clients, you can hire an agency to handle the SEO work, content creation, and link building for you. This is a win-win situation – you can scale your business faster because you’re good at closing deals, and you have a partner handling the Whitelabel SEO, which is great for you.

Hiring a Whitelabel SEO agency provider is one of the pros of this approach. It allows you to leverage their expertise and resources while you focus on your core competencies, which can help you grow your business more efficiently.

4. No Need to Worry About SEO Tools

Many SEO agencies offer tools as part of their subscription or full service packages. These tools are essential for the reporting and analytics required for an SEO campaign. Without access to these tools, the agency would be unable to provide the necessary data and insights, such as search console information.

Therefore, it is crucial that the SEO agency has the appropriate tools to effectively manage and monitor the campaign. These tools are a fundamental component of the service they provide to their clients.

5. Affordable

As a reseller, you can still generate profits and earn revenue through this agency. There are numerous Whitelabel SEO agencies available, many of which offer very competitive rates. This ensures that you can continue to earn a viable income.

By partnering with a Whitelabel SEO agency, you can provide your clients with high-quality services while maintaining your own profit margins. These agencies handle the technical aspects of SEO, allowing you to focus on building relationships and growing your business.

The Cons When Hiring Whitelabel SEO Agency

1. Risk of Low Quality Work

There are some Whitelabel SEO agencies that cannot deliver the quality of results they promise. When considering hiring an agency, it’s important to check their portfolio, case studies, and testimonials on their website. You can also ask your colleagues in the SEO industry about the agency’s performance and the quality of their work for their clients.

Unfortunately, there are many agencies out there that cannot provide good quality work. It’s crucial to do your research and ensure the agency you choose has a proven track record of delivering excellent results for their clients.

2. They also Outsource it to Another Whitelabel SEO Agency

The reason why companies may outsource their SEO work to a Whitelabel SEO agency is that the agency likely does not have the internal team, expertise, or processes to fully meet the client’s needs. By outsourcing the work, the agency can still earn a profit while having the work done by a third-party provider, often in countries with lower labor costs.

When working with a Whitelabel SEO agency, it’s important to ensure they are not simply outsourcing the work to another agency. You can check their legitimacy by reviewing their website, verifying they have an in-house team, and looking for case studies or testimonials that demonstrate their capabilities. This will help you confirm they have the knowledge and resources to deliver on their promises, rather than just passing the work along to another provider.

3. Lack of Control

This is an important point that applies not only to Whitelabel SEO agencies but to any agency. If they are not focused on quality control and thoroughly checking their work, they cannot ensure its quality. This is especially true if they are using automated “black hat” tools that are difficult to control.

Even if an agency has a lot of clients, they need to make sure they can still handle the workload and maintain quality control. They need to have a good process and system in place to manage all the tasks and deliverables. As an agency grows, this becomes increasingly challenging, so a robust process is essential to sustain high-quality work.

To address this, agencies should have a well-trained team and strong management practices in place. This will help them avoid quality control issues and ensure they can continue to deliver excellent results for their clients as the business grows.

4. Bad Communication

There are agencies out there that may seem promising at first. When prospecting, they may follow up daily, using persuasive language to get you to subscribe to their services. However, once you’ve signed up, the communication often drops off completely. This lack of ongoing support is not ideal.

Before engaging with any agency, it’s important to ask about their communication process. Find out how they will stay in touch, what kind of support they provide, and what deliverables you can expect. This will help ensure you get the level of service and attention you need.

At our company, for example, we have a live chat feature that allows clients to ask questions anytime. We also have a dedicated customer support team and a project manager who oversees the work and communication throughout the process. Maintaining clear, consistent communication is crucial in digital marketing, as it helps ensure your project is handled properly.

5. No System and Process

If a company doesn’t have a clear process and system in place, they will struggle to generate revenue and acquire new clients. How can they effectively help you if they don’t have their own house in order first?
When hiring a Whitelabel SEO agency, it’s crucial to ensure they have a well-defined process and system.

Ask them about their approach – what is their process? What systems do they have in place? This is important because you’re looking for a long-term partnership, not just a short-term arrangement.

A reputable Whitelabel SEO agency should have their own internal processes and systems that they can then apply to your business. If they don’t have these foundational elements in their own company, they won’t be able to provide you with the best possible service and support. Look for an agency that can demonstrate their capabilities and show you how their approach will benefit your business over the long run.

6. Less Transparency

Transparency is a crucial concern, especially when we are not meeting face-to-face. Whether you are in the Philippines, the United States, Canada, Australia, or the UK, it is essential to have a clear understanding of the services or SEO offerings you are subscribing to.

You need to know the deliverables, the timeline, the frequency of reports (monthly, weekly), and whether there is customer support available in case of any issues. Transparency is vital when you are not seeing the service provider regularly or only communicating through online channels like Zoom.

Without this transparency, it can be challenging to trust the service provider and ensure that you are getting the value you expect. Therefore, it is crucial to have a clear understanding of the terms and conditions, the scope of work, and the communication channels before committing to any service or SEO package.

There are also white-label SEO agencies that specialize in working with local businesses. These agencies can be a great option, as they have expertise in helping local businesses succeed. If you’re a local business, you may want to consider working with a specialized agency like this, as they will likely be better equipped to meet your needs.

Similarly, if you’re in a specific industry, such as law firms, you may want to find an agency specializing in that industry. Agencies with deep experience in your industry will likely be better able to deliver the results you’re looking for.

However, even if an agency is specialized, you should still carefully evaluate its track record and ability to deliver results. It’s important to ask for examples of its work and ensure it can provide the outcomes you need, regardless of whether it is a generalist or a specialist agency.
